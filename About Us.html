Our Mission

The mission of Baguio Transport is very simple: Provide safe, reliable, and affordable work-related transportation services to residents of local communities within Cordillera Administrative Region(CAR). Although the transportation service is new, our Company is not. Baguio Transport is a new service being offered by Pogi Kami INC (www.pogikami.org), a 501 (c) 3 corporation. The Company has been providing transportation services to local residents of Cordillera Administrative Region(CAR) for nearly four decades! Baguio Transport is simply another service being offered to meet the transportation needs of our friends and neighbors.

Safety

Safety is our number one priority. That is not just a slogan. It is the most important element of our core mission. All Baguio Transport drivers are thoroughly screened prior to becoming employees of the Company. Once hired, each driver must successfully complete a comprehensive safety training program. Ongoing safety training is also conducted for all drivers. Random drug and alcohol testing is also an integral part of our overall safety program. Your safety is our responsibility, a charge we do not take lightly.

Reliability

We pride ourselves on meeting the needs of our clients. That includes not only getting you safely to your destination, but also getting you there on time. Each of our vans holds up to 13 people. Each vehicle is also equipped with a wheelchair lift to accommodate clients needing such assistance. The size of our vehicle fleet allows us to meet the specific transportation needs of our client base.

Affordability

The cost of each (one-way) trip within the region is ₱300. With the seemingly ever-increasing price of gasoline, utilizing Baguio Transport may actually save you money! It is also environmentally friendly, as our multi-passenger vehicles may result in fewer single occupant cars on the road.

Baguio Transport

Baguio Transport is a new transportation service available to residents of Baguio City, Nueva Ecija, La Union , and Pangasinan. If you live and work in one of these Locations, and need transportation to and from your place of business, you are eligible to use Baguio Transport.
